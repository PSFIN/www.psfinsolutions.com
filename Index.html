Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common credit repair questions

Is credit repair legal?
Yes, credit repair is completely legal. The Credit Repair Organizations Act (CROA) gives consumers the right to dispute inaccurate, outdated, or unverifiable information on their credit reports. We operate in full compliance with all federal regulations.
How long does the credit repair process take?
The timeline varies depending on the complexity of your case, but most clients see initial results within 30-60 days. Complete credit restoration typically takes 3-6 months, though some cases may require longer for optimal results.
What kind of score increase can I expect?
Results vary by individual case, but our clients typically see score improvements of 50-150 points. The actual increase depends on your starting score, the number of negative items, and the complexity of your situation. We focus on sustainable, long-term improvements rather than temporary fixes.
How much does credit repair cost?
We offer transparent, competitive pricing with no hidden fees. Our consultation is completely free, and we'll provide a detailed quote based on your specific needs. We believe in providing exceptional value without compromising on quality or results.
Why choose PS Fin Solutions over a local US company?
Our unique advantage lies in combining deep US credit expertise with operational efficiency. Our 20+ dedicated specialists focus exclusively on US credit laws and regulations, while our streamlined operations allow us to provide premium services at accessible prices. You get the same expertise as top-tier US firms with better value and personalized attention.
How do you ensure my data security?
We employ industry-standard security measures including 256-bit SSL encryption, secure file transfer protocols, and strict access controls. Our team is trained in data privacy best practices, and we maintain compliance with all applicable US privacy regulations.
What do I need to get started?
Simply schedule a free consultation with us. We'll guide you through obtaining your credit reports from all three bureaus (we can assist with this), and discuss your goals and timeline. No upfront fees or obligations required.
Do you work with all 3 credit bureaus?
Yes, we work with all three major credit bureaus: Experian, Equifax, and TransUnion. We also communicate with creditors, collection agencies, and other data furnishers as needed to resolve inaccuracies and disputes.
What types of items can you dispute?
We can dispute any inaccurate, outdated, or unverifiable information including: late payments, collections, charge-offs, bankruptcies, foreclosures, judgments, liens, identity theft items, and errors in personal information. We cannot remove accurate, verifiable negative information.
